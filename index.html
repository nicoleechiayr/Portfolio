Job Application AI Agent
An end-to-end automation that monitors, classifies, and logs job application responses — with zero manual effort.
Gmail Zapier GPT-4o mini Prompt Engineering Telegram

The Problem

With a Gmail inbox flooded with promotional emails, newsletters, and unrelated offers, it became increasingly difficult to spot and differentiate genuine job application responses. Important replies risked being buried and missed entirely during an already stressful job search.

The Solution

Built an AI-powered automation agent that watches Gmail 24/7, uses an LLM to intelligently classify every incoming email as job-related or not, and instantly pushes a structured alert to Telegram — with status, company name, and a plain-English summary.

The Outcome

Zero manual effort required to stay on top of application responses. Every reply is automatically classified and summarised, with an instant Telegram notification — making the job search process significantly more organised and stress-free.
